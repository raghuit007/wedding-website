Our Story

With the right person, you dont have to work so hard to be happy. It just happens effortlessly.

  • How I met her!

    They say, love just happens. Nope. That's in movies. Love is a process that needs patience, time and effort. Being in the same college started our love journey. She was intelligent and does not talk much. He makes a lot of noise and roams around with a herd of friends, but when he saw her, the world around him froze. He did not immediately know she was the one, but his heart surely skipped a beat.

  • The First Date

    Some time in 2013

    Every love story is beautiful, but ours is my favourite. First dates are generally nervous but we felt an instant connection. A type of feeling you get when you have spent years with a person. We laughed so hard that day that it remains the best date ever. We never knew that this day is going to be momentous and life changing for both of us, but hey, what is life if everything can be expected or anticipated. Often the unconventional paths result in the best experiences of life. This date was just the beginning of a long journey ahead of us.

  • The Yes date!

    October 2018

    Nothing comes easily in life. Our story would not have been this beautiful if not for the things we encountered in this journey. Time and circumstances seperated us, yet our love endured it all. Like a phoenix that raises from the ashes, we hit low many times in our journey but we persisted and always rose. Finally on the day when we knew we are going to be togther for the rest of our life, it was easily one of the best and remarkable days. Our love has taught us a lot more about the world than the world combined together and all is well that ends well!

  • The D date!

    February 9 2019

    When you realize you want to spend the rest of your life with somebody, you want the rest of your life to start as soon as possible. On this day, we will be pronounced man and wife and we cant wait for you to come be part of our celebration called love. We solicit your gracious presence on the occasion of our marriage. We seek wishes and blessings for the long road ahead.
